Create a modern, elegant, highly visual multi-page website for a company called Zest.

This is not an ecommerce website for checkout, but it should feel structurally similar to a premium Shopify-style site with:

detailed brand pages
detailed product pages
image galleries
rich product information
strong storytelling
a B2B / Hospitality section
a bespoke solutions section
enquiry/contact calls to action throughout

The website must feel:

modern
minimal
natural
personal
premium without being old-fashioned luxury
consumer-beautiful while still serving B2B customers underneath

The visual direction should combine:

the clean structure of Salt & Mud
the product depth of Nikura product pages
the polished calm of NEOM
the softness and imagery feel of Miniml
a fresh and colorful identity inspired by the Zest logo

Do not make it look corporate, generic, techy, or like a basic distributor catalog.

Core Brand Direction

Zest is a family-led importer and distributor of natural beauty, wellness, spa, and lifestyle products.

The company’s values are deeply personal:

every customer matters
every conversation matters
every relationship matters
every supplier relationship is selected and nurtured with care
products are chosen with purpose, not just to make money
the business is built on trust, service, flexibility, and long-term relationships
Zest has been established for 23 years and should feel credible, trustworthy, and enduring

The site should communicate that Zest is:

thoughtful
values-led
highly curated
flexible
customer-focused
experienced
warm and personal
commercially capable without feeling salesy

The website should suggest that Zest helps improve wellness experiences through carefully selected natural products and tailored solutions.

Primary Website Goals

The website must:

Build trust and credibility
Tell the story of Zest in a believable and emotionally intelligent way
Showcase multiple brands beautifully
Support many products and future expansion
Include a strong B2B / Hospitality area
Show that Zest can create curated rituals and treatment concepts for hotel spas and wellness spaces
Feel rich in information, not like a thin brochure site
Generate enquiries via contact forms and WhatsApp
Leave room for future downloadable brochures, testimonials, and journal/blog content
